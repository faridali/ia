QUESTIONS





How does this program work?

We'll first explore what kind of web app you want to build. You'll then receive fun video tutorials to get you comfortable with Ruby on Rails. After that you'll dive straight into developing your web app. Your personal mentor will guide you for 1 hour each week via live screen sharing. You can ask questions at any time on our support page during the 4 weeks.




How much time do I have to dedicate?

This is completely up to you. We reccommend at least 10 hours a week. The more hours you put in, the more you'll learn.




Will 4 weeks be enough to build a web app?

A simple web app can be built within 4 weeks. Even if you don't finish building your web app, we will connect you with the right tools so that you can continue building after this program is finished. If you need more time with mentors, you can always subscribe to another 4 weeks.




What is your refund policy?

We'll provide you with a $300 refund if you drop out within the first week.
